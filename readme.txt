== Description ==
WC Min Max Quantities for WooCommerce is an extension that enhances control over your sell. It enables you to set a special rule over the cart total and also the number of products. You can also set special rule for individual products too.
 
How Min Max Quantities for WooCommerce will benefit you:
*	Make custom packages for various occasions.
*	Special deal for any particular product.
*	Force people to buy more along with your best seller.
*	Maximize your benefit.
*	Increase total sales volume.

The features:
*	Limit the total quantity of products in cart.
*	Limit the total cost in the cart.
*	Limit total quantity of a single product.
*	Exclude item from your defined rule.
Note: If you set rules over any individual product then it will overwrite the global rule for itself, but everything else will follow the global rule.

 
== Installation ==
 
1. Upload `wc-minmax-quantities` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
